Northeast Robotics

About Robotics

Northeast Robotics derives itself as a STEM Club. Students will develop a variety of skills to create robots that compete in competitions.

What To Expect With Joining

Team Cooperation

Team members will work together to achieve the best possible result.

Time Investment

Team members willingly have to be able to invest time into the club.

Adopting and Learning Skills

Team members will learn skills that will help aid in competitions.

Competitive Readiness

Just like any sport or esport, the drive to be competitive and win is of upmost key. Members should be ready to deal with challenges that come ahead in competitions.
